about us

Ehsaas is a new Sauparnika Ayurveda brand dedicated to ​providing exceptional postnatal care for mothers and their ​newborns. Our services are based on the ancient wisdom of ​Ayurveda, ensuring a healthy and balanced recovery after ​childbirth.

At Ehsaas, we offer personalized care packages ​that include traditional Ayurvedic treatments, specially ​made medicines, and custom diet food. We aim to support ​the physical and emotional well-being of mothers and their ​babies.

Our experienced team provides care at your home or in our comfortable facilities. We offer 24-hour nursing care, nutrition advice, and psychological support to ensure you and your baby receive the best care possible.

Choose Ehsaas for our nurturing and holistic approach to postnatal care. Let us help you embrace motherhood with confidence and peace of mind, knowing that we are here to support you every step of the way.

our vision

To be the leading provider of holistic post-maternity care, ​empowering mothers and nurturing newborns through ​ancient Ayurvedic wisdom, ensuring their health, happiness, ​and well-being.

services

Healthcare Center ​Services

The hospital offers a variety of services, including Abhyangam (head and body massage), face and body packs, Kashaya Kuli, Rasnadi Thalam, Doomapanam, Kesha Doopanam, Anjanam, Udara Vestanam, and Siro Dharam. Additionally, they provide yoga and meditation sessions. Postpartum yoga and meditation are particularly beneficial for new mothers, as they can improve mood and energy levels, enhance sleep quality, reduce stress and anxiety, increase strength and flexibility, and foster emotional bonding with the newborn.

Home Services

Our home services include Abhyangam (head and body massage), face and body packs, Kashaya Kuli, Rasnadi Thalam, Doomapanam, Kesha Doopanam, Anjanam, and Udara Vestanam. We also offer Ayurveda doctor consultations, psychological assessments, and mental support for postpartum issues. Treatments and medicines are provided under the supervision of an Ayurveda doctor. Please note that our Ayurveda Panchakarma therapists do not handle domestic chores such as laundry, cooking, or babysitting. For Siro Dhara, clients need to visit the hospital. These services are available in and around the Manjeri locality.
